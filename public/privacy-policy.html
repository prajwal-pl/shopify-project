Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ring Builder ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Shopify app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information from Shopify

When you install our app, we collect:

  • Store name and Shopify domain
  • Store owner email address
  • Product information (titles, prices, images) for products you mark as ring settings or diamonds
  • Order information when customers purchase custom rings

1.2 Information from Customers

When customers use the ring builder on your store, we collect:

  • Ring configurations (selected setting, diamond, metal type, ring size)
  • Customer email addresses (if they save, share, or submit inquiries)
  • Customer names and phone numbers (if provided in inquiry forms)
  • IP addresses (for security and fraud prevention)

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Ring Builder functionality
  • Process subscription billing
  • Send you important service updates and notifications
  • Respond to customer inquiries submitted through the ring builder
  • Improve our app and develop new features
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your information. We share information only in these circumstances:

  • With you (the merchant): Customer inquiries, configurations, and engagement data
  • With Shopify: As necessary to provide the app functionality
  • Service providers: Email services (SendGrid), hosting providers, analytics tools
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Data stored in Shopify metafields (survives app uninstall)
  • Application database with encryption in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use our app. When you uninstall the app:

  • Shopify metafields remain on your products (you control these)
  • Application database records are deleted after 48 hours
  • Customer inquiry data is deleted after 90 days or upon request

6. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Export: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@your-domain.com or use the GDPR data request features in your Shopify admin.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Session cookies: To maintain your login session
  • Preference cookies: To remember customer view preferences (grid/list)
  • Analytics: To understand app usage and improve features (aggregated data only)

No third-party advertising cookies are used.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

  • Support: support@your-domain.com
  • Website: your-domain.com
